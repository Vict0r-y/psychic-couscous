My Ninth Grade Project!

My ninth-grade project was focused on increasing my baking skills. Throughout my 9th grade year I made several types of cookies, not only to test out different types of cookies but to track my progress as a baker. I had some of my peers taste-test these cookies and provide ratings as well.

- Pumpkin Cookie! -

For this project I decided to use a control. I did this by picking a random week I did not have time to cook and bring a professionally made cookie to better track my recipes. As such I bought Harmon's pumpkin cookie as I knew people liked it.

This cookie had a score of 8/10

Image source: https://lifemadesimplebakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/Copycat-Harmons-Pumpkin-Chocolate-Chip-Cookies-680x680.jpg

- Stained Glass Cookie -

Cookie Recipe: https://www.delish.com/holiday-recipes/christmas/a37691142/stained-glass-cookies-recipe/

This was the first cookie I had made for my project, and it shows as it is one of the lowest rated. I wish I had gone into making this cookie at the end of my project instead of the beginning as at this point, it might have been only the third time I had ever made cookies. The cookies weren't bad, however the jolly rancher tasted off as I chose grape. Some of my reviewers said it tasted like alcohol is all I will say about that.

This cookie had a score of 6.3/10

- Cashew Cookie -

This cookie was an easier cookie with less complexity, and it is a family recipe. This cookie is one of the ones I am prouder of as it is one of my firsts while also being one of the better ones. I used a bit more help in this cookie from my parents, however I did learn important skills all the same. I was a bit concerned about the nuts included in the cookie, however no one was allergic to cashew nuts.

This cookie had a score of 7.9/10

- Black & White Cookie -

Cookie Recipe: https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/a32934916/black-and-white-cookies-recipes/

This was one of my better cookies, coming in third place. I got this recipe from a delicious recipe. The frosting was bit hard as it required me to put in water, and I ended up overwatering my frosting. I ended up having to borrow powdered sugar to complete this recipe. Looking back, this cookie probably got third place from how big I made these cookies.

This cookie had a score of 8.5/10

- Oatmeal Cookie -

This was a good cookie overall; I did burn a batch of these cookies but besides that these cookies were good. This was the first cookie I made alone, and it does affect the rating however, it was nice to not rely on my parents as much though.

This cookie had a score of 7.2/10

- Sugar Cookie -

Cookie Recipe: https://www.melskitchencafe.com/the-best-swig-sugar-cookies/

I usually wouldn't want to make a cookie like this because they are quite generic however, for this cookie I did it in collaboration with some of my friends! We did a baking class together the week before and were excited to decorate some cookies. I was tasked with making the cookies so we could decorate them. For this recipe however, the recipe was drier, and I did not really know how to fix that. (Which is adding a teaspoon of water at a time until it has an ideal texture and then cook it for longer) As such, the cookie was undercooked and was watered down. I did have a fun time decorating these cookies. However, when we went to serve them, it had been a couple of days and the cookies have gone bad due to the circumstances.

This cookie had a score of 5.8/10

- Carmel Cookie -

>This was one of my best tasting cookies that I ended up making myself. However, with this cookie I put the cookies sideways inside of my container overnight, where the caramel inside dripped out and it stuck together. I do not know where I got this recipe from. Fun fact I did this recipe because of a game of truth and dare. These cookies tasted good though.

This cookie had a score of 8/10

- No Bake Cookies -

This type of cookie is one that I had made before this project and is a family recipe. Me and my brother loved making this type of cookie as it usually needs two people to complete. This cookie was so good that I almost brought it to the presentation but then I got nervous about allergies. I experimented with this cookie making and tried making some almond flavored, however I never brought those in. This cookie got fourth overall.

This cookie had a score of 8.4/10

- Brownie -

You may ask, are brownies even cookies? I did not think so either, however I wanted an excuse to make brownies and the people in my class wanted brownies. However, I found out that brownies are different from cookies, and I added too much cocoa, as such it had more of a rich taste. That paired with its questionable cookie status got it last place.

This cookie had a score of 5.5/10

- CinnamonRoll Cookie -

Cookie Recipe: https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/recipes/a44700/cinnamon-roll-cookies-recipe/

For this cookie, it was like making a cinnamon roll. If you want a cinnamon roll and don't really want to have the time investment, I suggest making these as they are easy, and I made this type of cookie multiple times. Also, these cookies made me experiment with different types of cinnamon rolls.

This cookie had a score of 8.5/10
